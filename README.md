# IEEE_Conference_Paper_Generator

🚀 Built something useful for researchers and students!

I’ve developed an **IEEE Conference Paper Formatter** — completely free, browser-based, and no installation required.

🌐 **Try it here:** (https://abhishek-metraskar.github.io/IEEE_Conference_Paper_Generator/)

✨ What it does:
• Write your content and see it rendered **live in exact IEEE two-column A4 format**
• Supports **MS Word–style editing** — bold, italic, headings, tables, figures, and more
• **One-click PDF download**, automatically named after your paper title

The goal was simple: make IEEE formatting **fast, accurate, and hassle-free**.

Give it a try and let me know what you think — your feedback would mean a lot! 🙌
